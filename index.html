04 — Case Study · AI Automation

Invoice Processor — Claude-powered AP agent

A production automation that reads incoming vendor invoices (PDF), extracts structured data with Claude, routes the correct bank account by vendor-plus-entity, writes to a Notion AP Tracker, archives to Dropbox, and prepares a journal entry for Sage — all without human data entry.

How it works

01 · INGEST

Vendor PDFs arrive via email or Dropbox upload. A Make.com scenario fingerprints the file and dispatches it to the extraction queue.

02 · EXTRACT

Claude parses the PDF and returns structured JSON: invoice number, vendor, destination entity, line items, totals, taxes, due date, bank details.

03 · RECONCILE

The bank account is resolved by the vendor + destination-entity key. The invoice number is checked against the tracker to prevent duplicates.

04 · REGISTER

A new row is written to the Notion AP Master Tracker with all fields, relations to vendor and bank databases, and a link to the archived PDF.

05 · ARCHIVE & PREP

PDFs are filed by entity + month in Dropbox. A Sage-ready CSV is generated for batch journal-entry import.
